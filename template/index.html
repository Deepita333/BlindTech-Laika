Frequently Asked Questions

Q. How can the Virtual Assistant help Blind Individuals to learn Computer Skills?

Blind users may study hands-free thanks to the virtual assistant's usage of AI and machine learning, which enables voice commands and gesture detection.

Q. What kind of Computer Skills are covered in the educational modules?

Basic subjects including Introduction to computers (CPU, Mouse, Keyboard), Basic Software Usage (Email, Web Browsing), Keyboard skills, and Navigation are covered in the instructional modules.

Q. Is the platform accessible for the Blind Users?

Yes, the platform is made with blind people's accessibility and usability in mind. It includes features like keyboard navigation, high contrast images, and compatibility with screen readers.

Q. How does this platform track user progress?

The platform keeps track of users' progress by evaluating how well they complete interactive exercises and courses, and it modifies lesson plans according to each user's unique learning style.
